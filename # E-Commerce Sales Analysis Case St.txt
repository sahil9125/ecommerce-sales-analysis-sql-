# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Case Study

## Scenario
An e-commerce company stores data about customers, products, orders, and order items in PostgreSQL.

## Tables
- customers
- products
- orders
- order_items

## Questions
1. Display how many orders each customer has placed and total amount spent.
2. Find which city generated the highest sales.
3. Find average price and total sales per category.
4. Display customers who spent more than ₹50000.
5. Show order summary of last 10 months.
6. Identify most sold product by quantity.
